Write a program to get collection of numbers from user and save it in a list. Remove duplicate numbers from the list.

And, display numbers in below order:

a. Divisible by 3
b. Divisible by 5
c. Even or Odd

Input:
20,21,34,55,20,37,42,34

Output:
20 is divisible by 5
21 is divisible by 3
34 is even number
55 is divisible by 5
37 is odd number
42 is even number
